Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF compressor free to use?

Yes, our PDF compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges.

Is there a file size limit?

You can compress PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, consider splitting them first.

How much can I reduce my PDF file size?

Depending on the content and settings, you can typically reduce PDF size by 30-70% without noticeable quality loss.
